<p><p>The bridge building tower cranes market includes several types, each designed for specific construction needs. Self-erecting tower cranes are compact and utilized for smaller projects, easily assembling themselves. Flat top tower cranes offer a streamlined design without a towering profile, ideal for urban environments. Hammerhead tower cranes feature a horizontal arm for increased lifting capacity, suitable for large-scale projects. Luffing jib tower cranes possess a versatile jib that can adjust its angle, making them effective in tight spaces and close to other structures.</p></p>
