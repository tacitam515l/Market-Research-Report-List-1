<p><p>The Glass Bulb Sprinklers market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Pendent sprinklers hang from ceilings and distribute water downward, ideal for open spaces. Upright sprinklers are mounted on pipes with water spraying upward, suitable for areas with high ceilings. Horizontal sidewall sprinklers are designed for wall mounting, effectively protecting narrow spaces. Concealed sprinklers remain hidden until activated, offering aesthetic appeal while ensuring fire safety, making them popular in commercial and residential environments.</p></p>
