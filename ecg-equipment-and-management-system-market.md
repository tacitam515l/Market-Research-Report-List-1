<p><p>The ECG Equipment and Management System market encompasses various types of devices used for cardiovascular monitoring. Holter monitors provide continuous recording of heart activity over 24-48 hours. Resting ECG systems are designed for quick, stationary assessments of heart health. Stress ECG monitors evaluate heart function under physical stress. Event monitoring systems record and analyze heart rhythms during specific events, allowing for targeted diagnostics. Together, these technologies enhance cardiovascular care through improved monitoring and diagnosis of heart conditions.</p></p>
