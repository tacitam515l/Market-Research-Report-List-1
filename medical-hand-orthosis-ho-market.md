<p><p>Medical Hand Orthosis (HO) refers to devices designed to support, align, or correct the function of the hand and wrist. The market is divided into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics allow movement while providing assistance, often used in rehabilitation to promote functional use of the hand. Static orthotics, on the other hand, immobilize or restrict movement to protect or stabilize the hand in cases of injury or surgery. Both types cater to diverse therapeutic needs, enhancing recovery and functionality.</p></p>
