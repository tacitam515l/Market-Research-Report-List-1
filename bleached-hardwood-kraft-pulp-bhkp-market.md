<p><p>Bleached Hardwood Kraft Pulp (BHKP) is a key raw material used in the production of high-quality paper and board products. The market is typically divided into Northern Bleached Hardwood Kraft (NBHK) and Southern Bleached Hardwood Kraft (SBHK). NBHK is sourced from northern hardwood trees, known for their longer fibers, resulting in stronger paper. SBHK, obtained from southern hardwood trees, offers a different fiber composition and is generally more cost-effective. Both types cater to varying industry needs for strength and quality in papermaking.</p></p>
