<p><p>Stenting and dilation devices are medical tools used primarily to open narrowed or blocked blood vessels and other pathways in the body. Stents are small mesh-like tubes that are inserted into arteries or other structures to keep them open, ensuring proper blood flow. Dilation devices, such as balloons, are used to temporarily expand these pathways, often in conjunction with stenting. Together, these devices are crucial in treating various cardiovascular conditions, including coronary artery disease and peripheral artery disease.</p><p>The stenting and dilation devices market is experiencing significant growth due to an increase in the prevalence of heart diseases and a rising elderly population susceptible to vascular issues. Innovations in materials and designs are enhancing the efficacy of these devices, further propelling market expansion. Additionally, advancements in minimally invasive surgical techniques are boosting their adoption rates. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period, driven by technological advancements and increasing awareness of cardiovascular health.
