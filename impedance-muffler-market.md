<p><ul><li>Expansion</li><li>Resonance Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impedance muffler market is primarily divided into two types: expansion and resonance mufflers. Expansion mufflers operate by enlarging the exhaust flow area, reducing pressure and noise through increased volume, making them effective for general applications. Resonance mufflers utilize sound wave interference, designed to cancel specific noise frequencies, enhancing sound quality in performance applications. Both types serve distinct purposes in managing exhaust noise and enhancing engine performance across various automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
