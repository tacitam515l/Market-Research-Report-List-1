<p><p>The industrial-grade magnesium chloride market primarily consists of two types: anhydrous magnesium chloride and hexahydrate magnesium chloride. Anhydrous magnesium chloride is a dry, highly soluble form, commonly used in chemical manufacturing, road de-icing, and dust control. Hexahydrate magnesium chloride, on the other hand, contains water molecules and is often employed in applications requiring hydration, such as in fertilizers and animal feeds. Both types serve various industrial purposes, depending on their physical and chemical properties.</p></p>
