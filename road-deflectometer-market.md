<p><strong>The Road Deflectometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Falling Weight Deflectometer (FWD)</li><li>Light Weight Deflectometer (LWD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road deflectometer market includes two primary types: Falling Weight Deflectometer (FWD) and Light Weight Deflectometer (LWD). FWD utilizes a weight dropped from a specified height to measure pavement deflection, providing insights into structural integrity and load-bearing capabilities. In contrast, LWD employs a lightweight drop weight for quick and portable assessments, making it suitable for field applications. Both tools are essential for evaluating pavement conditions, guiding maintenance strategies, and ensuring road safety and performance.</p></p>
