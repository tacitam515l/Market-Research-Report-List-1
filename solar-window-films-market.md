<p><p>The Solar Window Films market includes three main types. Clear (Non-Reflective) films allow natural light while reducing UV exposure without altering building aesthetics. Dyed (Non-Reflective) films provide color and privacy, absorbing solar energy to reduce heat gain without reflective properties. Vacuum Coated (Reflective) films, on the other hand, enhance energy efficiency by reflecting solar radiation, offering superior heat rejection. Each type serves specific needs, balancing light transmission, aesthetics, energy efficiency, and privacy for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
