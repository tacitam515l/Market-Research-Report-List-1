<p><p>Neurosurgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential devices used in surgical procedures to cut tissue and coagulate blood vessels. The market for ESUs is divided into three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which utilize a single electrode and require a grounding pad for electrical return; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, where both electrodes are positioned near the target tissue for precise energy delivery; and Vessel Sealing Units, specifically designed for sealing blood vessels through controlled thermal energy, ensuring hemostasis during surgeries.</p></p>
