<p><p>The motorcycle brake pads market primarily consists of two main types: sintered and ceramic brake pads. Sintered brake pads are made from metal particles fused together through heat and pressure, providing excellent durability and performance in high-temperature conditions, making them ideal for sport and off-road motorcycles. In contrast, ceramic brake pads are composed of ceramic fibers and other materials, offering quieter operation, reduced dust, and improved performance in standard riding conditions, making them popular among casual riders and commuters.</p></p>
