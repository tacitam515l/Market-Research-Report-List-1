<p><ul><li>Hydroxyl Terminated</li><li>Carboxyl Terminated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder polyester resins are categorized mainly into two types: hydroxyl terminated and carboxyl terminated. Hydroxyl terminated resins contain hydroxyl groups that allow for better adhesion and crosslinking with curing agents, making them suitable for applications in coatings and adhesives. Carboxyl terminated resins, on the other hand, have carboxylic acid groups that enhance compatibility with other substances and provide improved flexibility and durability, making them ideal for powder coatings and thermosetting applications. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
