<p><ul><li>Surface Safety Valves</li><li>Subsurface Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pneumatic Safety Valve market includes two main types: Surface Safety Valves and Subsurface Safety Valves. Surface Safety Valves are installed on the surface of oil and gas operations, providing immediate response and control during pressure fluctuations. In contrast, Subsurface Safety Valves are located below the surface, designed to prevent uncontrolled fluid flow from the wellbore. Both types are crucial for ensuring operational safety, protecting personnel and equipment, and minimizing environmental risks in energy production.</p></p>
