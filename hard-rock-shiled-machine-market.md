<p><p>The Hard Rock Shield Machine market is primarily divided into two types: Shielded Type TBMs and Open Type TBMs. Shielded Type TBMs are designed to excavate hard rock formations while providing support to the tunnel face, minimizing ground settlement and enhancing safety. In contrast, Open Type TBMs are more suitable for less stable ground conditions, relying on external support systems during excavation. Both types cater to various project requirements, including infrastructure development and underground construction in challenging geological environments.</p></p>
