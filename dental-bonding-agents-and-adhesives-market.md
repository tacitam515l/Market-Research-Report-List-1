<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Bonding Agents and Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total-etch Bonding Agent</li><li>Self-etch Bonding Agent</li><li>Universal Bonding Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental bonding agents and adhesives market consists of three primary types: total-etch bonding agents, self-etch bonding agents, and universal bonding agents. Total-etch bonding agents require the selective etching of enamel and dentin, providing strong adhesion. Self-etch bonding agents simplify the process by combining etching and priming, reducing sensitivity and application time. Universal bonding agents are versatile, functioning as either total-etch or self-etch, offering greater flexibility for clinicians. Together, these agents enhance dental procedures' effectiveness and longevity.</p></p>
