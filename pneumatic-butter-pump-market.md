<p><p>A Pneumatic Butter Pump is a specialized device designed for transferring and dispensing butter, often used in commercial kitchens and food processing facilities. These pumps utilize compressed air to create a vacuum, facilitating the movement of butter from storage containers to production lines or serving areas. Their efficiency and reliability make them ideal for handling viscous materials like butter, ensuring consistent flow and minimizing waste.</p><p>The Pneumatic Butter Pump Market is projected to experience significant growth, with expectations of a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period.
