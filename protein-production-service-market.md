<p><strong>The Protein Production Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prokaryotic Expression Systems</li><li>Mammalian Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Yeast Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Protein Production Service market encompasses various expression systems tailored for producing recombinant proteins. Prokaryotic expression systems utilize bacteria, like E. coli, for rapid and cost-effective production but may lack post-translational modifications. Mammalian cell expression systems offer complex protein structures and glycosylation but are more expensive and time-consuming. Yeast cell expression systems bridge the gap, providing moderate costs and some post-translational modifications. Other systems include insect cells and cell-free systems, catering to specific protein production needs.</p></p>
