<p><p>Fuel cell power generation systems are categorized into portable and fixed types. Portable fuel cells are compact, lightweight units designed for mobility, making them ideal for applications like backup power, remote locations, and personal electronics. In contrast, fixed fuel cells are installed in a stationary setting, supplying consistent power for residential, commercial, or industrial needs. The fixed type often serves larger energy demands and integrates into the grid, while portable types prioritize convenience and versatility in power supply solutions.</p></p>
