<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital control valve positioners are essential components in automated control systems, ensuring precise positioning of valves. The market is segmented into single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners use pneumatic pressure for one-directional operation, typically relying on a spring for return, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, double acting positioners utilize pneumatic pressure for both opening and closing actions, providing greater control and faster response times, ideal for applications requiring higher reliability and accuracy.</p></p>
