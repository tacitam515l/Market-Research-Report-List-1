<p><p>The hydraulic pressure sensors market includes two primary types: differential and relative sensors. Differential type sensors measure the pressure difference between two points, making them ideal for applications requiring precise pressure monitoring in varying conditions. Relative type sensors, on the other hand, measure pressure against a reference atmospheric pressure, providing a straightforward assessment of pressure in systems. Both types cater to industries like manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, offering critical data for system performance and safety.</p></p>
